Tips and Tricks for Optimal Results


Isolated Character

Make sure there really is a single character in the image you upload, and that the character does not touch the edges of the image.

Head-on View

The algorithm can tolerate small angles, but a head-on view of the character will provide optimal results.

Only Two Colors Allowed

Best results will be obtained when the character and the background are of different but uniform colors.
