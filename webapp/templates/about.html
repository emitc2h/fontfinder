About


FontFinder was conceived in the context of the Insight Data Science Program, during the 2016B session. I felt the problem of searching for fonts wasn't solved adequately by the existing tools, and that a solution based on deep learning might be more powerful than other approaches.

See the demo presentation.

My name is Michel Trottier-McDonald and my background is in particle physics. I took part in the search for the Higgs boson from 2008 to 2014. I was directly involved in finding evidence that it couples to leptons. This project was done in the context of my transition from academia to industry.
